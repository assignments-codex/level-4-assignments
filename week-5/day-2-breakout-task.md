#### Goal

Build the create side of a full stack app.

#### Steps

1. Complete what we built in lecture.
2. Add a controlled form in React.
3. Send the form data to Flask.
4. Save the new record in the database.
5. Show the new record in the app after submit.

#### Extra practice if you want

- Reset the form after submit.
- Show a success message.
- Add one more field to the form.
